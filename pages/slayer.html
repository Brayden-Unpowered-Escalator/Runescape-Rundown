Slayers make use of different pieces of unique gear to deal with unique problems across the world.




Perhaps you need a Mirror Shield to help fend off the gaze of a Basilisk
or
A Leaf Bladed Spear that will rend the flesh of the creatures known only as Turoth.
or
A Face Mask for your face so the whipping sands of a Dust Devil don't choke you out before you have a chance to even fight.
Slayer is a skill that assigns Task's to adventurers who are tasked with eliminating large numbers of monsters and other problems across the world of Gielinor.

Masters of Slayer will assign you and other adventurers various 'Slayer Tasks' in all different manners of areas, from dark and dank caves to active volcanoes.
From lost crypts to uncovered cities. It could be anything on four legs, perhaps a

small bird

or even a dragon, or maybe it is on two legs and looks just like you and I.




Regardless, if a Master has deemed them a task worth taking care of you will be tasked to see it through.
If you have a problem that can't be handled by conventional handymen, there is a high likelihood a Slayer can take care of that for you.

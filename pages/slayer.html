Slayer isn't like those other skills!

Slayer synergizes with all Combat Stats!
This includes Strength, Attack, Defence, Ranged and Magic.
as well as supplementing your Prayer skill with many bones and ashes for you to utalize towards your prayer training!

During your slaying you will also find metal items that can be turned into Coins through the use of Alchemy spells!
Too add to your spoils of war, you will also recieve powerful unique drops off of some monsters!
These will greatly aid your further endevours and any Slayer worth their salt should seek these out!

As with all skills, after reaching level 99 in the skill you earn a Skill cape!

This is true for the Slayer Cape as well execpt for one crucial factor.
This cape has a super cool radical icon on it, the Slayer Icon!

Slayers make use of unique gear to deal with unique problems.




Perhaps you need a Mirror Shield to help fend off the gaze of a Basilisk
or
A Leaf Bladed Spear that will rend the flesh of the creatures known only as Turoth.
or
A Face Mask for your face so the whipping sands of a Dust Devil don't choke you out before you have a chance to even fight.
Slayer is a skill that assigns Task's to adventurers who are tasked with eliminating large numbers of monsters and other problems across the world of Gielinor.

Masters of Slayer will assign you and other adventurers various 'Slayer Tasks' in all different manners of areas, from dark and dank caves to active volcanoes.
From lost crypts to uncovered cities. It could be anything on four legs, perhaps a

small bird

or even a massive fire breathing Dragon.





Regardless, if a Master has deemed them a task worth taking care of you will be tasked to see it through.

From Rats and Goblins and bears a Such.
To Dragons and Demons and Spectres with their foul Touch.

If you have a problem that can't be handled by conventional handymen, there is a high likelihood a Slayer can take care of that for you.
